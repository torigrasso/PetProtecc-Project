Benefits of Adoption

Many are wary of the longstanding effects of adopting an animal rather than purchasing one from a chain or a breeder. Below are a list of benefits that will undoubtedly relieve you of any doubt you may have!

  • The cost of adoption is drastically lower than that of purchasing.
  • Most animals who have been housed in shelters have already received their vaccines and are neutered/spayed.
  • You are helping erradicate puppy mills instead of funding their future abuse of innumberable animals.
  • You'll always have someone that loves you unconditionally; waiting for you to come every day.
  • Pets are a great way to connect with new people who share a passion for the love of their fur baby.
  • You will be saving the life of an animal that is capable of bringing you an immense amount of laughter and bed time cuddles.
