These Animals Deserve a Second Chance

Of the 3 million cats and dogs euthanized in shelters each year, approximately 2.4 million (80%) are healthy and treatable and could have been adopted into new homes. One of the top ten reasons pets are surrendered is for behavior. What's more is that according to a study done in 2018, up to 96% of all dogs turned in had not recived behavior training.
You could be the opportunity that these hopeful animals have been waiting for. Go find your new loving companion now. If you're not quite sure yet, take our quiz here.
